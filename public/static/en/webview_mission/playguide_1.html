How to Play Panel Missions

Clear missions and open all of the panels on the sheet to read an exclusive story.
Also, you can get enhancement materials, gems, and various other items by completing the missions.

Note: There may be special conditions required to complete missions. Check conditions using the details button on each panel.

All Missions Completed Reward

Complete all the missions on each sheet to receive a reward.

Note: Missions will be carried over from the last time the event was available, so players will not be able to obtain mission rewards for missions they have already completed.
Note: Some mission requirements have been updated.
Note: Missions that have been updated will still be counted as complete for players who have completed the missions during the last time the event was available.
Note: Rewards obtainable from missions have not changed.


Reward for Completing All Missions on Each Sheet

Sheet 1: Gems x100, exclusive ★★★★ weapon Faith x1
Sheet 2: Gems x500
