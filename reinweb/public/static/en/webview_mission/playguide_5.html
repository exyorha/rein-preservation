How to Play Panel Missions

Clear missions and open all of the panels on the sheet to read an exclusive story.
Also, you can get weapons and various other items by completing the missions.

Note: There may be special conditions required to complete missions. Check conditions using the details button on each panel.

All Missions Completed Reward

Complete missions to earn the event-exclusive ★★★★ weapon Jubilant Spirit and items such as Piece: Black Pearl.
Up to five Jubilant Spirits can be earned by completing all the missions on every sheet.
