About Recollections of Dusk

Recollections of Dusk are high-difficulty quests in which you can see special character stories, and get limited-time costumes and weapons.
Each level consists of multiple quests. Costumes and weapons used to complete quests cannot be used in other quests in the same level.

These quests can be challenged at any time.
Recollections of Dusk quests for each character will be added periodically.

About Chamber of Dusk

Chamber of Dusk is an area where you can challenge up to five doors of special quests.
The quests can be accessed in any order from their respective doors, except for the central door, which can be accessed after all of the other quests have been completed.

Quests can be accompanied by two types of restrictions: One which specifies characters that must be included in your deck, and one which specifies required affinities, which relate to the proficient affinities that correspond to each costume.

Once a quest has been completed, the costumes and weapons included in the deck used to complete it cannot be used for any other quests on that same floor.
If you find yourself stuck, you can reset the clear status of a quest and challenge it again with different costumes and weapons.

* There is one exception to this rule: On floors with restrictions that stipulate required affinities, weapons alone can be included in decks used for multiple quests within a single floor. * Resetting a quest's clear status means you will have to clear it again.
* Resetting a quest's clear status will lock the central door if you have previously unlocked it, and you will be unable to play it.
* Fulfilling the clear conditions again will unlock the central door again.

About Area Burdens and Barrier Skills

An area burden is a status which confers disadvantageous effects to the player. These effects are active throughout an entire battle (though there are some that are active for only a limited amount of time) and come in two types: those that affect everyone, and those that affect just your party. To overcome these disadvantages, one should equip weapons possessing barrier skills that counteract area burden effects. Note: The burden effect could also be time limited depending on the type of area burden/ There are different ranks for barrier skill and the burden effect could be reduced individually for each character based on the total rank of equipped weapons.
Please check for more information regarding this reduction feature in the details of Area Burden on the screen confirming quest participations.

Note: Barrier skills will take effect from both main and secondary weapons included in the loadout.
Note: The rank of barrier skills could not be enhanced.
Note: Barrier skills cap at rank 10, and its effects will remain the same even if the rank exceeds the cap.

About new Character Stories

A new Character Story will be unlocked and become available each time you complete a Chamber of Dusk quests.
The story will be added to the Library's "EX Character Stories" tab.

Note: Stories will be added only by completing normal quests.

About Costume and Weapon Enhancements

Earn exclusive costumes and weapon enhancement materials by completing quests in "Normal", "Hard" and "Very Hard".
The collected materials could be used to enhance costumes and weapons.

Note: You would be able to earn all materials required for ascension, awakening, evolution for costumes and weapons by completing the difficulty "Very Hard".
