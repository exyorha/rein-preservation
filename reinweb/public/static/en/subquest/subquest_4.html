Dark Memory

Dark Memory is a special sub-quest type that can be unlocked by completing each chapter of the Main Quest on Hard difficulty.
Completing each chapter on Hard difficulty will earn you a special weapon.
By evolving this special weapon to its final stage, you can get a special character.

Weapon Manifestation

In certain evolutionary stages of special weapons, a special process called manifestation occurs.
When manifested, the special weapon's appearance will change and a special story will play.

The evolution (manifestation) of special weapons requires special materials.
These materials can be obtained mainly through the Dark Memory sub-quests, which include the EX Summon Ticket Quests, Dark Coin Quests, Dark Trial, and Dark Lair.

Quest Types

EX Summon Ticket Quests

From these quests, you can obtain both common and special weapon-exclusive evolvement materials, as well as EX Summon Tickets that allow you to use the Dark Memory Chapter Summons. Fulfill the unlocking conditions to unlock quests with higher difficulties and more rewards.


Dark Coin Quests

You can get items like Dark Coins to exchange for items in the Dark Coin Exchange. Fulfill the unlocking conditions to unlock quests with higher difficulties and more rewards.


Dark Lair

You can challenge the Dark Lair once a day.
You can get a lot of items with a single attempt, as well as rare materials needed to evolve special weapons.


Dark Trial

The evolutionary stage of your special weapon determines the difficulty of the Dark Trial boss quest. The higher the evolution stage, the more difficult the trial becomes.
By conquering the Dark Trial, you will be able to unlock higher levels of the EX Summon Ticket Quest, Dark Coin Quest, and Dark Lair.
Acquire a special character by completing the top-level Dark Trial, which is unlocked after the final manifestation of the special weapon.

Dark Memory Chapter Summons

The Dark Memory Chapter Summons have been added to chapter summons.
To use the Dark Memory Chapter Summons and get the materials needed to enhance special weapons, you'll need EX Summon Tickets, which come as battle rewards in Dark Memory quests.

Dark Coin Exchange

The Dark Coin Exchange has been added to the exchange.
The Dark Coin Exchange allows you to exchange Dark Coins that drop in the Dark Memory Quest for material needed to enhance special weapons.
